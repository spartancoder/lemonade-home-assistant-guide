# Introduction

Welcome to the Lemonade Home Assistant Guide. This guide will help you set up a complete local voice assistant system.

## What is Lemonade Server?

Lemonade Server is a local AI server that provides speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and and language model capabilities. All processing happens on your local machine, ensuring complete privacy and control.

## Why Local Voice Assistant?

- **Privacy**: Your voice data never leaves your local network
- **Control**: You choose your AI models and customize behavior
- **No Subscription**: Run entirely offline after initial setup
- **Hardware**: Use your AMD NPU for acceleration
